Light Duty Towing


Chatham Towing specializes in high-quality light towing services for a variety of vehicles, including cars, SUVs, motorcycles, and other light-duty vehicles..


Heavy Duty Towing


Our specialty lies in heavy towing, where we excel in transporting vehicles of all sizes with precision and car. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring the safe.


Recovery Towing


We specialize in recovery towing services to assist vehicles that are stuck or involved in accidents. Our team is highly trained to handle these situations with precision and care, always prioritizing safety.

Flatbed Towing


Our flatbed trucks feature advanced technology and are operated by experienced professionals dedicated to ensuring your vehicle’s safety throughout the towing process.

Lockout Assistance


We’re proud to offer our Roadside Assistance services, including expert Lockout Assistance. If you’re locked out of your vehicle, there’s no need to worry—Chatham Towing is ready to provide fast, professional help to get you back on the road.

Storage


At Chatham Towing provide secure storage solutions for vehicles of all sizes. Whether you require short-term or long-term storage for a car, truck, motorcycle, or any other vehicle, our facilities are equipped to meet your needs.
